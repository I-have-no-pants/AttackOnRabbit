Attack on Rabbit v 1.0

A first person flight cyberpunk flight simulator for Oculus Rift.
Bunnies are having a party, ruin it!

Made during LIU Spring gamejam 2015. The theme was Disruptive Rabbits.

Use joystick to play or keyboard.

Keyboard bindings:
Ctrl - Fire
WS - Speed
AD - Yaw
Up-Down - Pitch
Left-right - Roll

ESC - Pause
Space - Restart
K - toggle Oculus / normal camera

Known bugs: 
Runs in fixed resolution
Won't start on monitors with less than 800 resolution
